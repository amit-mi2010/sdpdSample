About Shri Aadishakti Maa Pateshwari Shaktipeeth


Shri Aadishakti Maa Pateshwari Shaktipeeth Mandir is one of the most sacred and ancient Shakti Peeths in India, located in Tulsipur town of Balrampur district, Uttar Pradesh. The temple holds immense religious significance among devotees of Goddess Durga, who is worshipped here as Maa Pateshwari Devi.

It is believed that this temple is among the revered 51 Shakti Peeths, where a part of the divine body of Sati fell when Lord Shiva carried her body across the universe. According to ancient scriptures and local traditions, the right shoulder (Pat) of Goddess Sati fell at this sacred place, giving the temple its name Devipatan.
